Our Subsidiaries

At the heart of every success, lie our subsidiary companies.

Brand Recognition

Akilinn organizes subsidiaries to keep while keeping its brand identities separate. This allows each brand to maintain its established goodwill with customers and vendor relationships. Subsidiaries are often used in acquisitions where Akilinn intends to keep the target company's name and culture. Subsidiaries also helps Akilinn to position part of its business as an alternative to the parent company at a different price point.Some of services conducted by Akilinn to its subsidiaries are:

  • Initial Branding and Marketing
  • Business environment setup
  • Market analysis and business planning
  • Hiring and training of staff
  • Provision of Quality control frameworks
  • Ethical and legal advice
